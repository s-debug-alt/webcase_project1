Room Description:

The Adventure Villa Room is a magical space designed to ignite the imagination and provide a fun, safe environment for kids. With vibrant colors, playful details, and interactive features, this room turns every stay into an unforgettable experience for children. The room is designed to keep kids entertained while offering the perfect retreat after a day of adventure.

Room Amenities

  • Beds: twin-sized bed with fun, colorful bedding.
  • Bathroom: Kid-friendly features like a low step for easy access to the sink and a child-safe tub with shower curtains featuring their favorite characters or animals. Kid-sized bathrobes and soft towels. Non-slip bath mats and bath toys provided.
  • Entertainment: 40-inch Smart TV with family-friendly channels, cartoons, and kid’s movies. Interactive games console (Nintendo Switch) with age-appropriate games. A selection of board games and children’s books. Bluetooth speaker for music.
  • Thermostat Control: Child-safe air conditioning and heating with adjustable settings to ensure the room is always the perfect temperature.
  • Mini-Fridge with Snacks: Mini-fridge stocked with kid-friendly snacks like crackers and In-room treats like homemade cookies.
  • Lighting: Night lights in the shape of animals, stars, or friendly characters. Fun, interactive ceiling light fixtures that project playful shapes on the walls and ceiling (such as dolphins, stars, or forest animals).
  • Safety Features: Childproof locks on windows and doors. Non-slip flooring and soft corner protectors for any furniture. A baby monitor or child safety gate available on request.
